Mauritian Nights

The west coast offers a unique maritime theatre. As we sail from Flic en Flac towards Le Morne Brabant, the mountain's silhouette creates a dramatic backdrop against the starlit sky.

Our route follows ancient trading paths, passing sheltered coves where sea turtles nest and crystalline lagoons that glow with bioluminescent plankton.

Each voyage is timed with lunar cycles—sailing during new moons for optimal stargazing, or full moons for romantic silver-lit waters.
